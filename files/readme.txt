Guidelines in publishing your website project
Go to http://jabry.net
Create your own account
Upload the files of your website
Note: Do not forget to rename your index file or homepage by "default.htm"
Ensure to upload all your files including css and javascript files
Submit the url of your website (through email: sonia@slis.upd.edu.ph)
Disclaimer: You can also subscribe to any web hosting sites. The goal of this activity is to learn how to publish created pages or websites.
